Frequently Asked Questions

How long does computer repair take?

Most computer repairs are completed within 24 hours. Simple issues like virus removal or software installation may be done the same day, while hardware repairs or data recovery may take 1-2 business days.

Is it worth repairing my old PC?

It depends on the age and condition of your computer. We provide free diagnostics and honest recommendations. Often, simple upgrades like an SSD or more RAM can dramatically improve performance at a fraction of the cost of a new computer.

Do you offer data backup before repairs?

Yes! We always recommend backing up your important data before any repair work. We can perform a complete backup as part of our service to ensure your files are safe during the repair process.

What brands of computers do you service?

We repair all brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, Gateway, Compaq, IBM, and custom-built computers. Our technicians are experienced with both Windows and Linux systems.
