Tablet Maintenance Tips

Use a Protective Case

Invest in a quality case with screen protection. Tablets are more prone to drops due to their size and weight.

Clean Screen Regularly

Use microfiber cloth and screen-safe cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage oleophobic coatings.

Manage Storage Space

Keep adequate free storage for optimal performance. Delete unused apps and move photos to cloud storage.

Battery Care

Avoid letting battery drain to 0% regularly. Keep tablet in moderate temperature environments when possible.

Update Software

Keep operating system and apps updated for security and performance improvements. Enable automatic updates when possible.

Gentle Handling

Hold tablets securely with both hands when possible. Be extra careful around children who may drop or mishandle devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does tablet repair take?

Most tablet repairs are completed within 1-2 business days. Simple repairs like charging port cleaning may be done same-day, while screen replacements typically require 1-2 days depending on parts availability.

Do you repair all tablet brands?

Yes! We service all major tablet brands including Apple iPad, Samsung Galaxy tablets, Microsoft Surface, Amazon Fire tablets, and more. If we don't have parts in stock, we can order them quickly.

Is it worth repairing an older tablet?

It depends on the tablet's age and repair cost. Tablets typically last 4-6 years with proper care. We'll provide honest advice about whether repair makes financial sense compared to replacement.

Can you save my data if the tablet won't turn on?

Often yes! Even if the tablet appears dead, we may be able to recover your photos, documents, and other data. Our diagnostic process will determine data recovery options.

Do you offer screen protectors and cases?

Yes! We stock quality screen protectors and protective cases for popular tablet models. Professional installation available to prevent bubbles and ensure perfect fit.
