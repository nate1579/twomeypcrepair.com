    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I schedule an on-site visit?

    Simply call us at (870) 257-4444 to schedule your appointment. We'll discuss your tech needs, provide an estimated timeframe, and arrange a convenient time that works for your schedule.

    What's the minimum charge for on-site service?

    We have a 1-hour minimum charge for all on-site visits. This covers the technician's travel time and initial diagnosis, even if the repair takes less than an hour to complete.

    Do you bring parts with you?

    Yes, we bring common parts and components with us for typical repairs. For specialized or expensive parts, we may need to order them specifically for your device and schedule a follow-up visit.

    What areas do you cover for on-site service?

    We provide on-site service within a 50-mile radius from Highland, Arkansas. This includes over 40 communities throughout North Arkansas. Check our service area page for the complete list.

    Can you set up my whole office network?

    Absolutely! We provide comprehensive business IT support including network setup, server installation, workstation configuration, and ongoing IT maintenance for small to medium businesses.
