Signs You Need Phone Repair

Screen Issues

  • Visible cracks or shattered glass
  • Dead pixels or discolored areas
  • Touch not responding properly
  • Display flickering or lines
  • Screen completely black

Battery Problems

  • Dies quickly after charging
  • Takes forever to charge
  • Phone gets very hot
  • Swollen or bulging battery
  • Unexpected shutdowns

Charging Issues

  • Charger doesn't fit properly
  • Only charges at certain angles
  • Charging is very slow
  • Phone doesn't recognize charger
  • Charging port has debris

Audio Problems

  • Can't hear during calls
  • Others can't hear you
  • Music/videos have no sound
  • Crackling or distorted audio
  • Volume buttons not working

Water Damage Signs

  • Phone got wet recently
  • Moisture under screen
  • Corrosion in charging port
  • Random restarts/freezing
  • Features stopped working

Camera Issues

  • Blurry or foggy photos
  • Camera app won't open
  • Black screen in camera
  • Focus problems
  • Video calls show black

Preventative Maintenance Tips

Use a Quality Case

Invest in a good protective case and screen protector. It's much cheaper than screen replacement.

Keep Ports Clean

Regularly clean charging port with compressed air. Avoid using metal objects that can damage connectors.

Manage Battery Health

Avoid extreme temperatures and don't let battery drain to 0% regularly. Use optimized charging when available.

Update Software

Keep your phone's operating system and apps updated for optimal performance and security.

Avoid Water Exposure

Even water-resistant phones can fail. Keep phones away from pools, sinks, and high humidity areas.

Regular Restarts

Restart your phone weekly to clear memory and refresh system processes for better performance.
