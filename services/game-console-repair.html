Common Console Issues We Fix

Overheating

  • Console gets very hot during use
  • Fan running loudly constantly
  • Unexpected shutdowns during gaming
  • Performance throttling or lag
  • Error messages about temperature

No Power

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does console repair take?

Most console repairs are completed within 2-3 business days. Simple issues like cleaning or software updates may be done same-day, while complex repairs like HDMI port replacement may take 3-5 days depending on parts availability.

Do you repair older consoles?

Yes! We service retro and vintage consoles including original Nintendo, Sega Genesis, Atari, and more. We can often find or fabricate parts for older systems that are no longer in production.

Can you fix disc read errors?

Absolutely! We can repair disc drives, replace laser assemblies, and clean/repair your scratched game discs. Many "unreadable" games and movies can be restored to working condition.

Do repairs void my warranty?

If your console is still under manufacturer warranty, we recommend contacting the manufacturer first. For out-of-warranty consoles, our repairs come with our own warranty and won't affect any expired manufacturer coverage.

What about overheating issues?

Overheating is very common and usually fixable! We clean internal components, replace thermal paste, repair or replace fans, and ensure proper airflow to keep your console running cool and quiet.
