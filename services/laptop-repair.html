Frequently Asked Questions

How long does laptop repair take?

Most laptop repairs are completed within 1-2 business days. Simple repairs like battery replacement may be done same-day, while screen replacements or motherboard repairs may take 2-3 days depending on parts availability.

Do you use genuine parts?

We use high-quality OEM-compatible parts that meet or exceed original specifications. All parts come with warranty and are tested for compatibility and reliability.

Is it worth repairing an old laptop?

It depends on the age, condition, and repair cost. We provide honest assessments and will let you know if a repair makes financial sense or if you'd be better served with a replacement.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer pickup and delivery service for $35 within our 50-mile service area. This is especially convenient for business customers or those with transportation challenges.
