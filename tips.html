Keep Your Devices Running Like New

Follow these expert maintenance tips from our technicians to extend your device's lifespan and improve performance.

Computer Tips

Keep your OS and drivers updated for security and performance improvements

Run antivirus scans regularly (we recommend Emsisoft for comprehensive protection)

Clean dust from vents every 6 months to prevent overheating

Don't ignore strange noises - could indicate failing hard drive or fan

Back up important files regularly using external drive or cloud storage

Restart your computer at least once a week to clear memory and install updates

Avoid installing software from unknown sources to prevent malware

Need help with your computer? Contact us for professional service!

Laptop Tips

Don't use your laptop on soft surfaces like beds or couches - this blocks ventilation

Calibrate your battery every few months (full drain then full charge)

Clean your keyboard with compressed air to remove dust and debris

Don't leave it plugged in 24/7 - this degrades battery over time

Get a good laptop bag with padding for safe transport

Keep liquids away from your laptop to prevent water damage

Need help with your laptop? Contact us for expert repair!

Phone Tips

Use a case and screen protector - they're the cheapest insurance for your phone

Don't charge overnight every night - it can reduce battery lifespan

Clear app cache regularly for better performance

Restart your phone once a week to clear memory and refresh performance

Keep at least 10% storage free for smooth operation

Avoid extreme temperatures (hot car dashboards, freezing cold)

Clean your charging port gently with a toothpick to remove lint

Need help with your phone? Contact us for quick repair!

Tablet Tips

Same screen protector advice as phones - protection is key

Update apps and OS regularly for security and new features

Reset network settings if Wi-Fi is slow or unreliable

Don't let battery drain to 0% regularly - it's hard on the battery

Need help with your tablet? Contact us for professional service!

Game Console Tips

Keep your console in a well-ventilated area away from walls and other electronics

Clean dust from vents with compressed air every 3 months

Don't stack things on top of your console - it blocks airflow

Use a surge protector to protect against power spikes

Store game discs in cases when not in use to prevent scratches

Keep the console in horizontal position when possible for better stability

Need help with your gaming console? Contact us for expert repair!

When to Seek Professional Help

Device won't turn on at all

Screen is cracked or has dead pixels

Battery swells or gets abnormally hot

Strange burning smell

Water damage (don't try rice - bring it to us immediately!)

Blue Screen of Death (BSOD) that keeps recurring
