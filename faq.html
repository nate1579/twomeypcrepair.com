General Questions

What services does Twomey PC Repair offer?

We offer comprehensive device repair services including computer repair, laptop repair, Mac repair, phone repair, tablet repair, and game console repair. We also provide website design services and on-site technical support for homes and businesses.

Where are you located?

We're located at 2379 HWY 62/412 Suite G, Highland, AR 72542. We're easy to find right on the main highway, with convenient parking available.

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We're closed on weekends, but you can always call and leave a message for emergency repairs.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome! However, we recommend calling ahead to schedule an appointment for faster service. This ensures we can give your device the attention it deserves without waiting.

What brands do you service?

We service all major brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, Apple, Samsung, Microsoft, Sony, Nintendo, Xbox, PlayStation, and more. If it's electronic and broken, we can probably fix it!

Repair Questions

How long do repairs take?

Most repairs are completed within 24 hours. Screen replacements are often completed the same day. More complex issues like motherboard repairs or data recovery may take 1-3 days, depending on parts availability and the complexity of the problem.

Do you offer free diagnostics?

Yes! All diagnostics are completely free with no obligation. We'll thoroughly test your device, identify the problem, and provide you with a detailed estimate before any work begins.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Absolutely! All repairs come with warranty coverage on both parts and labor. The warranty period varies depending on the type of repair, and we'll provide you with specific warranty details when you pick up your device.

Can I wait while my device is repaired?

For quick repairs like screen replacements and battery swaps, you're welcome to wait. For longer repairs that require extensive diagnosis or parts ordering, we'll call you as soon as your device is ready for pickup.

What if my device can't be repaired?

We provide an honest assessment of every device. If repair isn't cost-effective or technically feasible, we'll discuss replacement options with you. Since our diagnostics are free, you won't pay anything if we can't fix your device.

Pricing Questions

How much does a repair cost?

Repair costs vary by device and issue. We provide free estimates after diagnosis with transparent, upfront pricing - no hidden fees. Our goal is to offer competitive rates while using quality parts and providing excellent service.

Do you offer on-site service?

Yes! We offer on-site service at $75/hour for local areas (Highland, Cherokee Village, Hardy, Ash Flat) and $85/hour for extended areas within our 50-mile service radius. Perfect for network setup, multiple computers, or when you can't bring the device in.

What does pickup and delivery cost?

We offer pickup and delivery service for a flat fee of $35 within our service area. This is perfect if you can't make it to our shop due to transportation issues or time constraints.

Do you accept payment plans?

For larger repairs, we're happy to discuss payment options. Please contact us to discuss arrangements that work for your budget. We want to help get your device fixed without creating financial stress.

Data & Security

Will I lose my data during repair?

We take every precaution to preserve your data during repairs. However, we always recommend backing up important files before bringing in your device. We also offer backup services to ensure your data is safe before we begin any work.

Do you offer data recovery?

Yes, we can recover data from damaged or corrupted drives in many cases. Data recovery success depends on the type and extent of damage, but our technicians have specialized tools and experience to maximize recovery chances.

How do you handle my personal data?

Your privacy is our priority. We never access personal files beyond what's necessary for the repair. Our technicians are trained in data privacy best practices, and we take security seriously to protect your personal information.

Still have questions?

Our friendly team is here to help! Give us a call and we'll be happy to answer any questions about your specific device repair needs.
